We The Fest is an annual festival of music, arts, fashion, and food taking place in Indonesia’s capital of Jakarta. Since its inaugural edition in 2014, the festival has seen incredible performances from globally-known acts of different genres. This year, We The Fest is returning for its sixth edition on July 19 – 21, 2019, featuring American hip hop duo Rae Sremmurd, Australian DJ Anna Lunoe, and many more. Some of Indonesia’s most exciting musical acts will also perform at the festival, including Kunto Aji, Eva Celia, and Tulus. Book your tickets now for 3 days of unforgettable music performances!
